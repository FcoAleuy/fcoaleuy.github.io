Jesucristo Superstar

Starred as Jesus Christ in the production by Agrupación Andrómeda, one of Chile's most established musical theatre companies with 28 years of uninterrupted history. The 2026 season performed at the Gimnasio Municipal de Conchalí with a cast of 60+ artists, live musicians, and a first-class technical team — a full-scale rock opera with zero pre-recorded music.

Original project · 2024

GatoVox

Funk + Vintage aesthetics AI-generated music Instagram · TikTok

Founded and led GatoVox, an artistic project merging funk rhythms, vintage aesthetics, and feline-inspired themes with generative AI. The process involved street interviews that fed AI song creation — exploring the intersection of human stories and creative technology. Content gained significant traction on social media, bridging Francisco's artistic and tech careers.
