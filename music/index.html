Technology & The Arts

Where Art Meets Code

Two careers that rarely share the same résumé. Francisco has spent decades interpreting characters on stage — and just as long building software, databases, and digital products. That dual perspective shapes how he thinks about both disciplines.

The performing arts world has real, underserved IT needs: audience management, digital production tools, community platforms for independent artists, migration-related cultural projects. Francisco sees those gaps not as obstacles, but as opportunities — and has the technical range to address them.

🧭
SoyManada — Trusted directory for the migrant community

His most personal project: a digital platform that connects migrants with verified service providers — in their language, built with cultural empathy at its core. Technology that understands what it means to start over in a new country.
