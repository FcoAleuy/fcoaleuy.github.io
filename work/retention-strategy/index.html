Fraud Detection & Retention Strategy

Academic Capstone • Product Management • 2024

Context

As the final capstone for the Product Management specialization at Coderhouse, I developed a comprehensive strategy to reduce churn in a subscription-based model by identifying early signs of fraud and dissatisfaction.

Methodology

  • User Personas & Empathy Maps: Defined the “At-Risk User” profile to understand behavioral triggers.

  • Value Proposition Canvas: Aligned product features with customer pains (fraud vulnerability) and gains (security & trust).

  • Roadmap Definition: Created a phased rollout plan (MVP to V2) focusing on quick wins for retention.
