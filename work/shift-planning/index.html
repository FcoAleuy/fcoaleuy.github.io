Shift Planning & Fairness Tool

15 Park Bistro • Hospitality Tech • Product Owner

Context & Challenge

Managing shifts for a busy hospitality team was handled manually via Excel sheets and text messages. This led to last-minute confusion, perceived unfairness in shift distribution, and high administrative overhead for managers. There was no centralized “single source of truth” for availability.

My Role

I identified the operational bottleneck and acted as the Product Owner. I defined the requirements for a fairer system and coordinated with a remote development team to build a functional prototype.

The Solution (Proposed)

  • Smart Assignments: An algorithm to suggest shifts based on staff availability and skill level, reducing manager bias.

  • Transparency Dashboard: A real-time view for staff to see their hours and swap shifts seamlessly.
