Shift Planning & Fairness Tool

15 Park Bistro•Hospitality Tech•Product Owner

Context & Problem

Shift planning was handled manually through Excel sheets and fragmented messaging. This created inconsistency, data gaps, and unnecessary administrative load for managers.

  • ✕ Manual, error-prone scheduling processes.

  • ✕ Subjective shift allocation based on memory rather than data.

  • ✕ No link between occupancy demand and staffing levels.

  • ✕ Delayed communication leading to operational friction.

My Role: Product Owner

I identified the operational bottleneck and defined the product requirements. I prioritized features based on immediate operational impact (fairness & transparency) and coordinated with a remote development team to build a functional prototype.

Proposed Solution

  • ▹ Smart Assignments: Algorithm to suggest shifts based on staff availability and skill level.

  • ▹ Occupancy-Aware: Staffing levels adjusted automatically based on event data.

  • ▹ Real-Time Access: Cloud-based view for staff to see hours instantly.

  • ▹ Privacy-First: PIPEDA-aligned design with minimal personal data exposure.
